Terrorism Legislation

the terrorism legislation is a piece of law that can be used to prosecute any terrorist actions, these are defined by these following rules:

  • action designed to seriously interfere with or seriously to disrupt an electronic system
  • serious violence against a person
  • serious damage to property
  • endangering a person's life
  • creating a serious risk to the health or safety of the public or a section of the public
